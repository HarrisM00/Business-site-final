About Us

For over 25 years, Sure Shot Pest Control has been America's leading provider of pest control services. After two decades in the pest control industry, we are now setting higher standards in providing pest control services to our valued customers. Our operations include pest, odor, hygiene, and sanitation control services for residential, commercial, and industrial markets.

Sure Shot Pest Control is committed to protecting your family and your homes from unwanted pests. Over the years, people have learned that they can trust Sure Shot Pest Control for the protection of their property and businesses. We develop strong relationships with our customers — one of the prime reasons we continue growing while keeping our loyal clients.

Our team is trained and equipped for all types of pest problems. We are constantly adding new products and equipment, while also developing innovative ways to control pest issues. We are also expanding our staff to better serve our clients. We work hard so that you always have peace of mind.

Our Ultimate Goal

To establish our firm as the premier pest management service company. In doing so, we aim to earn the enduring loyalty of our customers by providing service that is recognized as superior value — ensuring profitability to our company and long-term rewards for our employees.

Mission Statement

Our employees are committed to achieving our ultimate goal by providing outstanding quality services and unsurpassed customer satisfaction through teamwork, empowerment, excellence, customer care, and innovation.
